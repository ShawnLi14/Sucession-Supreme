How to Play

Welcome to the game! Your goal is to build a climax community by carefully selecting and planting different plants.

Game Interface

The game interface consists of the following elements:

  • Goal Indicator: Located on the top left of the screen, the goal indicator shows your progress towards the next phase of succession.
  • Nutrient Level and Soil Percentage: Displayed on the top right of the screen, these indicators show the current nutrient level and soil percentage in the ecosystem.
  • Plots: You can click on the plots in the game to plant different plants and contribute to the ecosystem's development.

Building a Climax Community

To build a climax community, follow these steps:

  1. Select a plot by clicking on it.
  2. Choose a plant from the available options.
  3. Each plant will have a different nutrient cost.
  4. Repeat the process for other plots until you've created a diverse and stable climax community.

Remember to balance the different plant species and their needs to maintain a sustainable ecosystem. Be careful for droughts, which may make some plots uninhabitable for a period of time.

Difficulty

Currently, only the easy difficulty is in the game. The other difficulties may be added in the future.

Plants

Information about the plants follows:

Easy:
  • Lichen: Produces 10 nutrients/second for 15 seconds
  • Grass: Produces 50 nutrients/second for 6 seconds
  • Beans: Produces 100 nutrients/second for 15 seconds
  • Shrub: Produces 700 nutrients/second for 10 seconds
  • Hydrangea: Produces 750 nutrients/second for 20 seconds
  • Pin Cherry: Produces 6000 nutrients/second for 7 seconds
  • Spruce: Produces 5000 nutrients/second for 15 seconds
  • Pine: Produces 15000 nutrients/second for 10 seconds
  • Fir: Produces 20000 nutrients/second for 15 seconds
